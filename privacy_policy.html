American Imperial University Website Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("Policy") explains how American Imperial University ("us", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects the data you provide when you visit and use [https://americanimperialuniversity.com]("Website", "Service").

We reserve the right to update this Policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes, and we recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

What User Data We Collect

When you use our Website, we may collect the following types of data:

  • • Your IP address.
  • • Contact information, including your email address.
  • • Information about your interests and preferences.
  • • Data related to your online behavior on our Website.
Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for several reasons:

  • • To better understand and meet your needs.
  • • To improve our services and offerings.
  • • To send you promotional emails about products or services that may interest you.
  • • To contact you for surveys and market research.
  • • To personalize your experience on our Website based on your online behavior and preferences.
Safeguarding and Securing Your Data

American Imperial University is committed to protecting your data. We use advanced technologies and software to prevent unauthorized access, data theft, and disclosure. We strive to maintain the confidentiality and security of all information collected through our Website.

Our Cookie Policy

By using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies. Cookies help us analyze web traffic, understand which pages you visit, and improve your user experience.

The data collected by cookies is used solely to enhance your experience and is removed from our systems after statistical analysis. Cookies do not allow us to control your device.

Please note that cookies don't allow us to gain control of your computer in any way. They are strictly used to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not so that we can provide a better experience for you.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. For more information on how to do this, please visit https://www.internetcookies.com

Links to Other Websites

Our Website may contain links to external websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Restricting the Collection of Your Personal Data

If you wish to restrict the use or collection of your personal data, you may do so by:

  • • Checking or unchecking relevant boxes when filling out forms on our Website.
  • • Contacting us via email if you have previously agreed to share your information and wish to change that.

We will not lease, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties without your consent, unless required by law. Your data may be used for sending promotional materials if you have opted in to receive them.

Why choose an MSc in Data Analytics?

  • • Booming Industry Growth:The global data analytics market is experiencing rapid expansion, projected to reach $346.24 billion by 2030, with a CAGR of 30.1% from 2021 to 2030 (source: Allied Market Research). This explosive growth highlights the immense opportunities and demand for skilled data professionals.
  • • Endless Job Opportunities:According to the World Economic Forum's "Future of Jobs 2020" report, there will be a surge of 97 million job roles in data science by 2025. This surge contrasts with an expected 15.4% redundancy in traditional job roles, making data science a highly employable sector (source: World Economic Forum).
  • • High Demand, Low Saturation: The scarcity of skilled specialists in Data Science means that this sector is in high demand yet less saturated compared to other IT sectors. This scenario presents abundant opportunities for skilled professionals (source: World Economic Forum).
  • • Lucrative Earning Prospects: Data Science professionals are consistently among the highest-paid in the industry, especially in the United States. According to recent reports, Data Scientists can earn an average salary ranging from $100,000 to $150,000 annually, with senior roles commanding even higher salaries exceeding $200,000 (source: Glassdoor). This robust earning potential makes Data Science a highly sought-after and financially rewarding career choice in the US market.
  • • Versatile Career Growth: The applications of Data Science are widespread, spanning industries such as healthcare, banking, consultancy services, and e-commerce. This versatility provides opportunities for professionals to work in diverse fields, fostering professional growth and exploration.

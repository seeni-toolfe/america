Master of Business Administration in

International Business

Welcome to American Imperial University's MBA in International Business—your definitive path to global business mastery. In an era where global markets are intricately interconnected, our program equips you with the strategic acumen and cultural intelligence essential for dominating the international business landscape. Designed for aspiring leaders, this MBA program imparts a profound understanding of global business dynamics, preparing you to excel in a competitive global economy.

Why choose an MBA in International Business ?

  • • A Global Perspective: Gain invaluable experience working in a multicultural environment. Travel internationally, interact with diverse teams, and develop a global perspective on business challenges, international trade, and cultural negotiations, broadening your worldview.
  • • Enhanced Business Acumen: Learn to manage diverse teams, improve financial performance, and redesign business processes. Specialized courses in key areas equip you with essential knowledge and skills for the global economy.
  • • International Networking: Build a robust global network through interpersonal relationships. Strong professional contacts can lead to job opportunities and career advancements.
  • • Cultural Awareness & Global Mobility: Develop effective communication skills for engaging with international stakeholders. Navigate cultural norms successfully, enhancing your ability to operate in various global markets.
  • • Lucrative Earning Prospects: MBA graduates in the U.S. earn an average annual salary of $115,000, with substantial increases in industries like consulting, finance, and technology. Career growth can lead to average salaries ranging between $151,951 and $172,469 after three to five years of experience, plus comprehensive compensation packages including bonuses and stock options.​ Source: (Coursera)​​ (National University)​.

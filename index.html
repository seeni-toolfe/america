American Imperial University Gains Official Recognition

The American Imperial University (#12500) is officially recognized as an authorized autonomous degree-granting higher educational institution by the State of Florida Department of Education. This authorization implies that the institute significantly meets the preliminary standards and guidelines as laid out by the Commission for Independent Education (CIE), allowing it to operate as a private postsecondary university in the state of Florida. To verify the institute's recognition, please visit the Florida Department of Education's official website at https://www.fldoe.org.

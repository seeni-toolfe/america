An MSc in Data Analytics empowers individuals to unlock the power of vast datasets, shaping them into actionable insights. With this degree, professionals lead the charge in innovation, leveraging data to forge ahead in today's competitive landscape.

English Language Requirement :

• All students must speak, read, and write English fluently.

• International students not native in English must demonstrate proficiency with a composite score of 6 for IELTS or a TOEFL score of min 60 out of 120

Foreign Transcript Evaluations :

• Accepted from any agency that is a member of the National Association of Credential Evaluation Services (www.naces.org). Recommended agencies: Educational Perspectives (www.edperspective.org) and Josef Silny & Associates (www.jsilny.org).
